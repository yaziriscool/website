✦ Welcome to the official Elevator Game 64 wiki!

Elevator Game 64 is a fangame on Roblox based on Super Mario 64. It takes many inspirations from the Super Mario 64 rom hack, B3313. It is a combination of B3313, The Normal Elevator, vanilla Super Mario 64, and original stuff. The game has lots of areas to explore, from vanilla Super Mario 64 to original content created by the developers and contributors. Check out the pages if you are having trouble finding stars or if you want to know any mechanics the game has!
